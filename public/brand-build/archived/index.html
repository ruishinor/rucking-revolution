Brand Directions

Version A and Version B

Both directions keep the discipline and structure of rucking while removing military-coded friction. One is calmer and more book-native. The other is punchier and more campaign-native.

Version A - Quiet Momentum
Recommended

A warm, habit-building system designed to make rucking feel approachable, structured, and useful to ordinary adults. This is the strongest direction for beginner trust, article reading, and book conversion.
