# 05 Launch Plan

## Launch Principle

Use content to build trust, then let the ebook close the sale.

No paid ads are required in the first cycle. The launch should rely on:

- a clean beginner offer
- consistent Medium publishing
- a brand-new Instagram account using repeatable post templates
- one email capture asset on the website

## Recommended Funnel

Instagram and Medium -> website article or landing page -> free quick-start asset -> lead ebook -> next book or email sequence

## Primary Launch Asset

Free lead magnet:

`The First 3 Rucks - A One-Page Beginner Quick Start`

This should live on the site and be mentioned in every Medium and Instagram CTA for the first six weeks.

## Six-Week Launch Calendar

- Post 1: What rucking is, in plain language
- Post 2: The "I will look stupid" myth
- Post 3: You already have everything you need

Medium:

- Article: `The First 10 Minutes: How to Take Your First Ruck Today`

Instagram posts:

- How much weight should you carry?
- 3-point posture check
- 5-minute stretch

- Article: `How Much Weight Should You Actually Carry When Rucking?`

### Week 3 - Mistakes And Momentum

Instagram posts:

- what if I miss a day?

Medium:

### Week 4 - Social Proof And Belief

Instagram posts:

- calorie math
- 30-day transformation narrative
- before/after mindset carousel

Medium:

- Article: `The Calorie Math That Will Make You a Believer`

### Week 5 - Book Push

Instagram posts:

- cover reveal

Medium:

- Article: `You Already Have Everything You Need to Start Rucking Today`

### Week 6 - Launch Week

Instagram posts:

- launch day
- first reader reactions

- next-step post: what happens after day 30?

Medium:

- Article: `What Happens After Day 30? Your Next Steps as a Rucker`

## Instagram Setup Rules

Handle:

- `@ruckingrevolution`

Bio format:

- line 1: plain-language brand promise
- line 2: beginner focus
- line 3: free quick-start CTA

Suggested bio:

Weighted walking for ordinary adults.
Start simple. Build strength. Stay consistent.
Free quick-start guide below.

## Instagram Content Rules

- post in 4:5 whenever possible
- use 1 headline idea per post
- write captions like short coaching notes, not essays
- finish with one action only: save, share, or get the guide

## Medium Strategy

### Positioning

Medium should feel like the thoughtful public-facing voice of the brand.

### Author Signature

Use one short signature at the end of every story:

`Alexander writes about beginner-friendly rucking, consistency, and practical strength at Rucking Revolution.`

### CTA Pattern

End each article with:

- one line pointing to the free quick-start guide
- one line pointing to the lead ebook only after week 4

## Offer Ladder After Launch

### First Paid Offer

- `Your First Ruck`

### Second Offer

- `7 Beginner Rucking Mistakes`
  - expanded short guide or workbook

### Third Offer

- `Why Tracking Your Rucks Changes Everything`
  - tracker, logs, and habit system

### Leadership Expansion

- `How to Use Rucking Activities for Team Development`
  - separate audience branch

## Success Metrics

### Website

- lead magnet conversion rate
- article to product click-through rate
- product page conversion rate

### Instagram

- saves per post
- shares per post
- profile visits
- link clicks

### Medium

- reads
- read ratio
- follows
- clicks to site

## Launch Risk Notes

- Do not split attention across too many ebook titles before the first offer proves itself.
- Do not let Instagram become generic motivation content.
- Do not overuse tactical language because it reduces beginner conversion.
