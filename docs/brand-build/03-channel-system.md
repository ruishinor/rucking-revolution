# 03 Channel System

## Brand Architecture

The brand should behave like a single system across four surfaces:

- Website = trust and navigation
- Ebook = first paid conversion
- Medium = discovery and authority
- Instagram = repetition and identity

## Recommended Entry Offer

Lead with one flagship beginner ebook:

`Your First Ruck: A 30-Day Beginner Plan for Strength, Stamina, and Consistency`

Why this should lead:

- highest beginner intent
- strongest bridge from article content
- easiest promise to understand
- easiest to price and publish fast

## Website System

### Homepage Structure

1. Hero
   - one plain-language promise
   - one primary CTA to the lead ebook or starter guide
   - one secondary CTA to articles
2. Proof strip
   - beginner-safe
   - low-impact
   - no-gym
3. Start here module
   - 3-card system: Start, Learn, Track
4. Featured article
5. Featured book
6. Topic clusters
   - beginners
   - technique
   - consistency
   - community
7. Email capture
   - free one-page quick start or 7-day mini sequence

### Library/Product Page Rules

- show cover first
- show outcome second
- show format and length third
- never bury the buy button under long copy

### Article Page Rules

- clear estimated read time
- first paragraph under 55 words
- one trust-building proof block near top
- one in-article CTA
- one end-of-article CTA
- related article cluster at end

## Medium System

### Role Of Medium

Medium is not where the full sale happens.

It is where readers discover the worldview, trust the author, and decide whether the beginner book is worth buying.

### Medium Article Structure

1. Title with human tension
2. Short human opener
3. Fast explanation of the problem
4. Three to five practical points
5. One myth or mistake callout
6. One action block
7. One CTA to the site or book

### Medium Formatting Rules

- short paragraphs
- one short list every 300-500 words
- no more than one idea per section
- use one consistent author signature line
- use the same closing CTA language for 6-8 weeks

## Instagram System

### Role Of Instagram

Instagram creates recognition and repetition. It should make the brand feel active, useful, and consistent.

### Post Pillars

- Myth busting
- beginner mistakes
- posture and technique
- consistency and identity
- route or gear simplicity
- quiet community

### Template Set

Use six repeating templates only:

1. Myth card
2. Checklist carousel
3. One thing to do today
4. Progress tracker card
5. Quote or belief card
6. Photo plus practical caption

### Design Rules

- always include a strong first-slide headline
- keep slide copy large enough to read at arm's length
- use the logo mark only as a small footer or avatar
- prioritize saves and shares over decorative complexity

## Ebook System

### Interior Module Set

- chapter opener
- quick win checklist
- reality check callout
- milestone marker
- common mistake box
- technique diagram
- track-your-ruck worksheet

### Cover System Rules

- title must dominate at thumbnail size
- subtitle must explain outcome, not theme
- one focal mark or strong typographic treatment only
- keep clutter off the cover
- use high contrast between title and background

## Article Strategy

Write book-adjacent articles in clusters, not one-offs.

Recommended first cluster:

- The "I'll Look Stupid" Myth
- The First 10 Minutes
- How Much Weight Should You Actually Carry?
- 7 Beginner Rucking Mistakes
- What If I Miss a Day?

These five pieces pull readers directly into the lead book.
