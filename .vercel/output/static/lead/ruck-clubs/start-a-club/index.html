Start a Club

Introduction

Starting a ruck club is a rewarding way to build community, improve fitness, and share the benefits of rucking with others. This guide provides practical steps to launch your own successful ruck club.

Leadership Principles

  • Vision: Clearly define what you want to achieve with your club.
  • Planning: Develop a structured approach to launching and growing.
  • Communication: Keep members informed and engaged.
  • Adaptability: Be ready to adjust based on feedback and circumstances.
  • Service: Focus on providing value to your members.

Practical Checklist

  1. Research local interest and potential meeting locations.
  2. Define your club's purpose, values, and target audience.
  3. Set up a simple communication system (social media group, email list).
  4. Plan your first ruck event with a beginner-friendly route.
  5. Establish basic safety guidelines and equipment recommendations.
  6. Promote your launch through local networks and social media.
  7. Host your first ruck and gather feedback for improvement.
  8. Establish a regular meeting schedule based on member availability.
  9. Continuously evaluate and improve the club experience.
