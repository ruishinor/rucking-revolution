Endurance Rucking Challenge

Test your limits with this challenging endurance event featuring progressive distance increases over two days with varying loads and terrain.

Date: Aug 10, 2024 Location: Mountain Trail Network Duration: 2 days
Distance: 40 km Load: 15 kg Difficulty: Intermediate Price: €179.99 Max Participants: 30

Includes

  • Clearly marked course with checkpoints
  • Aid stations with nutrition and hydration
  • Medical support and safety personnel
  • Event t-shirt and finisher medal
  • Post-event recovery area with food and drinks
  • Digital timing and results

Requirements

  • Ability to complete 20km ruck with 10kg load
  • Basic self-sufficiency for outdoor activities
  • Own rucking equipment and hydration system
  • Completion of required liability waiver
  • Recommended: prior endurance event experience

Agenda

06:00 (Day 1) Participant check-in and bag drop
06:30 Mandatory safety briefing
07:00 Start wave 1: 20km Day 1 route
07:30 Start wave 2: 20km Day 1 route
12:00 First aid station (10km mark)
15:00 Second aid station and lunch (15km mark)
18:00 Day 1 finish line and overnight support
19:00 Evening meal and social gathering
06:00 (Day 2) Breakfast and gear check
07:00 Start: 20km Day 2 route
11:00 Midpoint aid station (10km mark)
14:00 Final aid station (15km mark)
16:30 Finish line celebrations begin
17:30 Awards ceremony and finisher medals
18:30 Event conclusion and departure
