Leadership Under Load Workshop

Develop leadership skills through practical rucking exercises that teach communication, decision-making, and team cohesion under physical stress.

Date: Sep 5, 2024 Location: Leadership Development Center Duration: 1 day
Distance: 8 km Load: 12 kg Difficulty: Intermediate Price: €129.99 Max Participants: 24

Includes

  • Expert facilitation by leadership and rucking specialists
  • Leadership workbook and reference materials
  • All meals and refreshments
  • Use of specialized training equipment
  • Personalized feedback and development plan
  • Certificate of leadership development completion

Requirements

  • Current leadership role or aspiration to lead
  • Basic physical fitness (able to walk 8km with load)
  • Openness to feedback and self-reflection
  • Completion of pre-workshop reading assignment
  • Own note-taking materials and water container

Agenda

08:00 Check-in, equipment issue, and introductions
08:30 Leadership principles and rucking connection
09:30 Form and equipment check
10:00 Leadership ruck: 2km with communication focus
11:00 After-action review and leadership debrief
11:30 Break and snack
12:00 Lunch
13:00 Decision-making under load exercises
14:00 Team problem-solving challenges
15:00 Feedback and communication drills
15:30 Break
16:00 Individual leadership assessment ruck (2km)
17:00 Personal feedback and development planning
18:00 Workshop conclusion and certificate awarding
