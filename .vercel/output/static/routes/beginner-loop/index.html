Beginner Loop

Perfect introduction to rucking with gentle terrain, clear markings, and multiple exit points.

Distance: 5 km Estimated Time: 1-1.5 hours Difficulty: Beginner
Elevation Gain: 50 m Load Recommendation: 10 kg Best Season: Spring, Summer, Fall
Terrain: Trail, Road

Notes

Well-marked trail suitable for all ages and experience levels. Dogs on leash permitted.
