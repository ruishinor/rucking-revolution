Urban Tactical Route

Mixed-surface route through urban environment practicing navigation, load transitions, and obstacle negotiation.

Distance: 8 km Estimated Time: 2-3 hours Difficulty: Intermediate
Elevation Gain: 100 m Load Recommendation: 12 kg Best Season: Spring, Summer, Fall, Winter
Terrain: Mixed

Notes

Be aware of traffic, follow pedestrian laws, and respect private property. Best attempted on weekends or off-hours.
